Welcome to Heelas

Heelas Health Care Services

Heelas Health Care Services is a trusted provider of comprehensive home health care in San Leandro, California, and surrounding areas. We are dedicated to helping individuals maintain their independence and quality of life in the comfort of their own homes.

With years of experience in the healthcare industry, our team of skilled professionals is committed to delivering compassionate, personalized care that meets the unique needs of each client.

Our Mission

To provide professional and paraprofessional services to clients in their homes, assisting them to achieve the highest level of potential in their day-to-day self-care activities. We are committed to providing high-quality, multidisciplinary care by professionals who recognize the need for comprehensive assessment of needs from both the client and professional's point of view.

Our Vision

To be one of the leading providers of a wide range of quality home health care services, recognized for enduring dedication to provide innovative, professional, and compassionate care to the communities we serve.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Quality

Delivering accurate, reliable, and compliant health services with integrity.

Compassion

Treating every client with dignity, respect, and genuine care.

Integrity

Upholding the highest ethical standards in all our services.

Excellence

Striving for excellence in every aspect of patient care.
