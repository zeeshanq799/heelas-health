Welcome to Heelas

Heelas Health Care Services

Our Mission:

To provide professional and paraprofessional services to clients in their homes assisting them to achieve the highest level of potential in their day to day self-care activities. We are committed to providing high quality, multidisciplinary care by professionals who recognize the need for comprehensive assessment of needs from both the client and professional's point of view.

Our Vision:

To be one of the leading providers of a wide range of quality home health care services, recognized for enduring dedication to provide innovative, professional, and compassionate care to the communities we serve.

Our Services

We provide comprehensive home health care services tailored to meet your unique needs and help you maintain independence at home.

Our Guiding Philosophy

Our goals and services are based on fundamental principles: the belief in the innate worth of every individual and the belief that each person, regardless of age, race, color, creed, sex, national origin or handicap(s) is entitled to maximize their potential as a human being and as a member of society.

We are dedicated to rehabilitating individuals within the confines of their residence, in order that they may maximize their contributions and fulfill their goals as a family member and member of society with a minimum of conflict.
